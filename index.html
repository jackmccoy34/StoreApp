    Bikes:

    Full Suspension

    Gritty machine to tackle the toughest terrain and get you down the mountain first

    Electric

    Cover more ground over less time with less effort

    Hardtail

    The workhorse of mountain biking

    Fat Bike

    Larger tires provide more traction on tough terrain with lower pressure to absorb impact

    Mullet

    A larger tire size in the front and smaller size in the rear. Usually 29" front and 27" rear

    Lefty

    Just one fork on the front tire results in less overall weight and easier handling
